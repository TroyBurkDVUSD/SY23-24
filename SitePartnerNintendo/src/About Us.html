About Us

Nintendo and all the experiences its given us will always live in my and my partners soul. Since we were young kids in the south side of Florida, me and my best friend Bryan were obsessed with our NES and Gamecube. My favorite was Kirby, and Bryans favorite was Legend of Zelda. What was both of our favorites though, terrifying trials, the awe - enducing sights, and the triumphs which sometimes we thought would never come. Of course, you can't play games forever and you've gotta do adult things. was sharing the awesome experience of playing those games with the entire neighbourhood. All the neighbourhood kids, the poor and rich ones, bonded together over the However later in life, freshly graduated from college, which was not all it was cracked up to be, me and my partner finally got together after 6 years over some beers and Legend of Zelda. And that's when it collectively hit us both what our real paths were. Like after trudging in the dank dungeon for years, we've finally found the ancient treasure with a satesfying jingle. We wanted everyone in the world to have the same life defining experience of Nintendo games,and so we thought the best way to do so was to make our own website. 20 years later, accomplishing our mission everyday, I feel that this was the right choice.
